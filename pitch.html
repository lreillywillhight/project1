Project 1 pitch: "Maze_Runner"

MAZE_RUNNER A primitive maze game (similar to Pac-Man). Player navigates a maze to the exit, where a new level is loaded. MVP is a basic maze to navigate. Stretch goals - multiple maps/environments, monsters to avoid, keys/doors, powerups, bonus items(treasure)/points system, graphics/sound/animation, longer levels (screen scrolling? multiple rooms?), hazards, puppies?
